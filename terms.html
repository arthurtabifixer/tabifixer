01Service Scope

Tabi Fixer is a personal travel concierge service operated by Arthur (the "Fixer," "I," "me") and designed to assist English-speaking travelers visiting Japan. Services include, but are not limited to:

  • Restaurant research and reservation assistance (phone bookings, inquiry emails)

  • Translation and interpretation of Japanese-language materials
  • Real-time support via messaging during your trip (SOS support)
  • Local recommendations for activities, transport, and hidden-gem experiences
  • Airport transfer coordination (when selected as an add-on)

All services are provided remotely unless otherwise arranged. This is a concierge and advisory service — Tabi Fixer does not act as a licensed tour operator, travel agent, or transportation provider.

02Response Times

Response times are the same across all plans. I'm available every day and aim to respond as follows:

  • 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM JST: Mostly within 30 minutes
  • Outside of those hours: Guaranteed within 2 hours

Japan Standard Time is UTC+9. If you're messaging from the US, that's roughly same-evening to early-morning your time — I'll still get back to you within 2 hours.

VIP plan clients have access to a direct phone line for urgent, real-time emergencies while in Japan.

03SOS Support Messages

SOS support is included with each plan (2 messages for Basic, 4 for Plus, unlimited for VIP). An SOS message is defined as a request for real-time help with an urgent or unexpected situation during your trip — for example, a missed train, a booking problem, a sudden illness, or a language emergency.

SOS messages are not intended for general trip questions, additional planning requests, or restaurant recommendation changes. Those are covered under your plan's standard scope.

Unused SOS messages do not carry over to future trips or plans.
